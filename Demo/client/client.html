Frequently asked questions

Drop us an email if you cannot find the answer here

Can I use Facetone for my Sales Team?

It is brilliant for your sales team. You can monitor your deal flows and at what stage each of your customers are at. Automated scheduling of calls and emails will help your sales team to close deals.

Can I use Facetone for my contact center?

Facetone incorporates all the functionalities for running a contact center. Facetone has amazing features for skilled based routing, call queue management, agent management including agent productivity, customizable IVR, supervisor centric dashboards and many more. It even has add-ons for wall board support.

What about using Facetone for my online chats from my website?

It works great, you can easily have chat enabled to your website. It is simple and what’s even more great is that you can also enable voice calling which can be an Internet call or a regular phone call back. With the advanced skill based routing you can ensure that requests from customers can be assigned to the right support person or team.

Is my data protected?

Your data is safe and are hosted in world class data centers from AWS, with the highest standards of security. We have built Facetone to hold a wide variety of confidential data and have done all that is required to protect your data. You can review our data usage and storage policy for a better understanding.

Can I bring my own voice line and use it with Facetone?

Absolutely, it is possible. All you need to do is to fill in the SIP information of your line on Facetone and you are ready to use.

Can I interface my current CRM to Facetone?

You can integrate many CRM using our open Integrations API or just use the pre-built connectors for Salesforce.com, ZenDesk, Zoho, Freshworks and many more.
